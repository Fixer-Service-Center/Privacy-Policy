Privacy Policy for Fixer

Effective Date: 12/25/2024

Welcome to Fixer, an app designed to help you monitor the state of your car, view car bills, and track repairs conducted at Fixer Center. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe experience while using our app. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information.

1. Information Collection and Use

Fixer does not collect any personal information from users. Our app is designed to provide information about your car's state and repair history without requiring personal data.

2. Data Storage and Security

All data related to car states, bills, and repairs is stored securely on cloud services. Access to this data is restricted to admin users only. Admin accounts are protected by a token system, and access requires a link sent to the admin's email, which must be used within one hour.

3. User Rights

If you need to access, update, or delete any information related to your car, please contact the admin. We are committed to ensuring that your data is accurate and up-to-date.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Fixer does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services. All data handling is managed internally within Fixer.

6. Children's Privacy

Fixer is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. Our app is designed for car owners in Egypt, where the legal age for car ownership is 18 years or older.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated to users through notifications within the app. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at fixer.car.service.center@gmail.com.

By using Fixer, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. Thank you for trusting us with your car management needs.
